Notification Addresses

Here you will configure your notification addresses. These are all of the possible addresses that APRS-Alert will send email to. Later, when you build your notification rules, you will select which address(es) will receive notification for each condition you define.
There are currently two types of notification: short and long. Short messages are intended for small-screen devices like mobile phones or pagers. A typical short message will look like this:
N4AC-9 is moving. 4.06 miles bearing 108.7 from Montgomery, AL A long notification will contain all of the information of a short message, and then some. A typical long notification message will look like this:
long notification, blah blah blah
